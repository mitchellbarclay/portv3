Hi, I’m Mitchell. I'm a digital designer based in Wellington, New Zealand.

My skills cover a versatile range of mediums, from animation and video design, graphic design and illustration, through to web design and front-end web development. I built this portfolio from scratch as a web-app to demonstrate this range. I am a creative at heart so I'm always looking at different ways to communicate my design vision.

I've worked as an in-house designer and Digital Media Manager for education company StudyTime since 2020, and have contracted as a freelance designer prior and during this time. I also have a double degree of a Bachelor of Design Innovation and a Bachelor of Commerce, majoring in Media Design and Information Systems respectively.

If you'd like to learn more, feel free to contact me, or check out my attached socials.
